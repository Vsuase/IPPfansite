The Despicable Me

The beginning

In a cheerful suburban neighborhood, surrounded by white picket fences and blooming rose bushes, there stands a black house with a lifeless lawn. Unnoticed by the neighbors, beneath this home lies a vast secret lair. Guarded by a small army of minions, we find Gru, plotting the most ambitious heist the world has ever seen: stealing the moon.
Equipped with shrink rays, freeze rays, and an array of battle-ready vehicles for both land and air, he defeats anyone who dares to challenge him. That is, until he encounters the unyielding spirit of three orphaned girls who, unlike anyone else, see something in him: the possibility of a father. The world's greatest villain is about to face his toughest challenge yet—three little girls named Margo, Edith, and Agnes.

Behold! A mischievous trio, transformed into tiny, rambunctious minions, have mysteriously appeared!
But who are they? Where did they spring from? And, the most pressing question of all—will they dare to steal the moon itself?
